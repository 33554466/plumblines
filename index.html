PROVENANCE reports only what the article itself provides. It does not verify whether a claim is true, and it does not hunt down sources on its own.
A named source is not the same as a good source — a stat from a lobby group is still "sourced." Judgment is yours.
"Dig deeper" suggestions are research starting points, not authoritative answers. Numbers from those sources should always be checked at the source itself.
